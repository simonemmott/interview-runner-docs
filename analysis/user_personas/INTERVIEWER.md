# Interviewer User Persona

An interviewer is a user of `Interview Runner` who wants to consistently interview 
candidates against a job specification.

They may define the job specification themselves or it may have been defined already.

They may want to define competency areas against which candidates can be interviewed.

They may want to define curated questions for one or more competency areas to be asked 
at interview.

They may want to define a candidate to be interviewed or they may exist already.

They want to create an interview (select job spec and candidate) or the interview may
exist already.

They want to be presented with `good` questions relevant to the candidates expressed
experience and the competencies of the specification.

They want to record the candidates responses to their questions.

They want a systematic report on a candidates fit for the job specification for which
the candidate was interviewed.
